Mr. Penumbra's
24-Hour Bookstore

Discover Timeless Classics

Mr. Penumbra has spent over 40 years scouring the earth for the most interesting books he could find. His careful work means that you get to enjoy carefully-curated books from every country of the globe.

From fairy tales to science fiction, we have something for every reader. Except the kind of people who read Twilight.

Enjoy Our Refreshing Café

We have delicious hot and iced espresso beverages, Lois Clary's fresh sourdough bread—a San Francisco icon—and more! Plus, frequent sippers can earn royalty points to redeem for free beverages or book credit later.

First time in the café? Enjoy a free drink on us. Just mention the code Unbroken Spine to redeem this offer.
